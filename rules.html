Rules

  1. Do not cheat the system.
  2. Screenshots are to be submitted in the Google Form provided. The screenshot must include the date, time and distance clocked.
  3. Treadmill submissions are not allowed.
  4. Participants may not make more than 2 submissions per day.
  5. Only the following apps may be used for mileage tracking: Strava, Runtastic, MapMyRun, Runkeeper, Nike+, Garmin, Endomondo, Fitbit, iPhone Health.
  6. Result submissions should be made on the same day as the activity.
