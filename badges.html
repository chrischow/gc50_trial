Running, and Cycling, and Swimming

Note: All distances indicated are pre-multiplier.



Ironman
The traditional Ironman Triathlon organised by the World's Triathlon Corporation consists of a 3.86km swim, 180km cycle and a 42km run. For this event, we simplified these distances to 42km of Running and 180km of Cycling and 4km of Swimming for our very own GC ironman.

Half Ironman
A badge for those aspiring to become the next GC Ironman. Complete 21km of Running and 90km of Cycling and 2km of Swimming to certify that you are still gunning for the Ironman award and are already halfway there.

Modelled after Tour de France, an annual cycling competition in France, you need to cycle 193km over the span of 50 days to earn this badge - the distance of the coastline around Singapore.
